Cafe Panic: Cooking game

Serve in your own coffee shop and join in the cooking game fever. Create new dishes and collect them in your cooking diary. Travel through different countries and become a world-famous chef. NEW ONLINE MULTIPLAYER! Challenge other players online and participate in the weekly tournament! MANY LEVELS AND HOURS OF FUN More than 700 LEVELS, 360 RECIPES, and 60 customers to complete and new seasons every month!. IMPROVE YOUR MENTAL SPEED Each level is a challenge for your mind, the number of customers and orders will grow and you will have to attend as quickly as possible. OPEN COFFEE SHOPS THROUGHOUT THE WORLD What are you waiting for to invest your money and open another Cafe in Japan or France? New recipes and decorations await you in each country. IMPROVE YOUR MACHINES Acquire new machines for your Coffee Shop, upgrade them, and give them maintenance. NEW RECIPES AND DISHES Learn new recipes and complex combinations. Your customers will pay more for a Unicorn Frappé, Kebabs, or Frozen Green Tea! CUTE DECORATIONS Add decorations and turn your cafe into a luxurious meeting point to eat delicious cookies or waffles! IMPROVE YOUR ADMINISTRATOR'S SKILLS Choose between a girl or a boy to train an expert and prepare the richest coffees and chocolates. MINIGAMES Take advantage of the bonus time by dropping coffee drops and collecting tons of prizes! MONITOR THE WI-FI AREA Fix the Wi-Fi zone when it fails and prevent people from going mad without paying! Check our social networks to find out about new content and events. FACEBOOK: https://www.facebook.com/cafepanic/ INSTAGRAM: https://www.instagram.com/cafe.panic/ Note: No internet connection is required to play.
